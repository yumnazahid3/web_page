About Me

I am a 1st year Computer Science PhD student at Stony Brook University where I am advised by Dr. Phillipa Gill.
Before coming to Stony Brook University, I completed my undergraduate degree from LUMS SSE where I worked with Dr. Ihsan Ayyub Qazi on Wireless Networks for 2 years.
